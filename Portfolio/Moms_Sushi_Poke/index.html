About Sushi & Poke

Fresh ingredients, friendly service, and meals made just for you.

Sushi & Poke is a welcoming local spot where guests can stop in for fresh sushi rolls, colorful poke bowls, and satisfying favorites made to order. Every visit should feel easy, warm, and dependable, from a quick lunch pickup to a relaxed dinner with family.

The menu highlights crisp vegetables, flavorful sauces, quality proteins, and friendly service that makes regulars feel remembered.
